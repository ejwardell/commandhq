Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

CommandHQ collects only the data necessary to provide analytics features and never shares your information with third parties.

Information We Collect

  • Game data you create (results, deck names, life totals, commander damage)
  • Device & app version info for support (anonymous)
  • iCloud sync metadata — encrypted and private

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide analytics
  • Sync across devices
  • Improve the app
  • Customer support

Information We DON'T Collect

We do not collect PII, contact lists, location, or advertising identifiers.

Your Rights

  • Export your data
  • Delete your data
  • Control iCloud sync
